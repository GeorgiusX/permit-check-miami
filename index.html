Chapter 18A — Landscape Requirements
§18A-6Minimum 15% of net lot area landscaped (residential); 10% (commercial/mixed-use)
§18A-7Street trees every 30 LF of frontage; min 2" caliper; from approved species list
§18A-8≥50% of required plants must be Florida-native species
§18A-9Parking lots: 1 shade tree per 10 spaces; 5% of parking area landscaped; islands min 5'×8'
§18A-10Buffer yards between residential and non-residential: Type B (5 ft) or Type C (10 ft)
§18A-11All landscaped areas require automatic irrigation with rain sensor
§18A-12Tree removal ≥4" DBH requires DERM permit prior to land clearing
§18A-13Screening hedges: min 3 ft at planting; reach 4 ft within 18 months
§18A-14Ground cover/sod established within 30 days of Certificate of Occupancy
§18A-15Preserved native trees receive landscaping credit
Miami 21 — Transect-Specific Standards
T3Min 60% of lot frontage landscaped setback; 1 tree per 3,000 SF; no parking in front setback
T4Min 30% frontage landscaped; 1 canopy tree per 40 LF; lawn panels min 4 ft wide
T5Street trees at 20–40 ft spacing; tree grates where sidewalk <8 ft
T6Street trees min 1 per 25 LF; continuous canopy on primary frontages; structured soil min 3 ft
CSMin 50% open/green space; canopy ≥30% of site; impervious surface ≤40%
AllSight triangle: no planting >30" within 10 ft of driveway/intersection; mulch min 3" depth
DERM — Tree Preservation & Environmental Rules
DERM-001Trees ≥4" DBH protected; removal requires DERM Tree Removal Permit
DERM-002Heritage trees ≥18" DBH require public notice and Board approval for removal
DERM-003Replacement: 1" DBH removed = 1" DBH replanted (on-site or mitigation fund)
DERM-004Certified arborist survey required if any trees ≥4" DBH on site
DERM-005Tree protection zone: no construction within 1 ft per inch DBH (min 5 ft); fencing required
DERM-006Invasive removal (Brazilian pepper, Australian pine, melaleuca) exempt; counts as credit
DERM-007Mangrove trimming requires DERM permit + DEP notification; removal prohibited
DERM-008ESL overlay requires additional environmental review; may require 25% upland buffer
DERM-009FLEPPC Category I or II invasive species prohibited in any landscape plan
